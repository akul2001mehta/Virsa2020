Turbanator

#virsa2020

Event Details

"In this market every head has a different fancy: everyone winds his turban in a different fashion."

Deciphering the word Turbanator, one can sense the pride with which the men wearing turban celebrate and carry the legacy forward. They have a unique style of dressing. It's what keeps them close to their roots and helps bring the diversity forefront.

Rules:

  • Only one entry per person is allowed
  • The participant should upload a video tying the turban(uncut video, Max- 15 mins)
    • The pre-tying (pooni, base) setup must be done before starting the video
    • Video must not include any kind of vulgarity, offensive elements which may hurt the religious sentiments. Such entries will be discarded right away
    • Video should be uploaded in mp4 format
    • Upload the video on google drive and share your drive link via google form. Ensure the use of Thapar Id in the form
  • The participant must upload 4 pictures of turban from different view i.e. Front, Back, Left and Right
  • The participant must upload his/her 2-4 complete photos in standing position
    • For complete photos any type or style of attire can be chosen:
      -Ethnic
      -Formal
      -Semi-Formal
  • Adhere to the deadline given i.e. 5th December
  • Disobedience or violation of rules will lead to disqualification
