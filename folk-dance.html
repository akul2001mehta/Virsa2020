Folk Dance

#virsa2020

Event Details

"Dance is the timeless interpretation of life"

A folk dance reflects the culture and life of the people of a certain region. Folk dance is the hidden langauge of soul and vernacular expression of culture.

Rules:

  • It should be a solo performances
  • Only one entry per performance is allowed
  • Participants must choose from any one of these 3 categories:
    -Bhangra
    -Giddha
    -Classical Indian (any indian cultural dance)
  • Participants are restricted only to Indian music
  • Time limit for the video is 40s to 1 min
  • Participants can use legitimate props like shikke/khunde/kaato etc. as per their requirements
  • Dress will not be a part of judgement criteria
  • Participants can enhance their videos with editing/transitions but dance steps should be visible clearly
  • There will be 2 rounds:
    -First Round - The candidates will be shortlisted by judges and will be eligible for participation in second round.
    -Second round - Entries of shortlisted candidates will be uploaded on our page for audience poll that will be having 10% judgment weightage and remaining 90% judgment is purely based on performance.
  • Entries will be accepted via a google form. Make sure to use your Thapar Id
  • Any kind of offensive content or vulgarity is strictly prohibited
  • Entries must be submitted before the deadline i.e. 5th December
  • Disobedience of rules will lead to disqualification
