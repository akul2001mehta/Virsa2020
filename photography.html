Photography

#virsa2020

Event Details

“Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still.”

Photographs have the power to create magic that words fail to create. It takes you to an illusionary wonderland and gives you the most surreal experience. Get ready to capture and create your own aesthetic illusions with Nazakat-e-Virsa!

Rules:

  • Only one entry per person is allowed
  • The theme for photography should be Indian culture and heritage
  • The format of the photograph should be .jpeg/.jpg/.png
  • Entry should be original and captured by the participant
  • The photo must be in its original state and cannot be altered in any way, including any type of editing
  • Your watermark/signature should be clearly visible in the image uploaded
  • Entries must not contain any material that has copyrights to a third party
  • Name of the file should be in the format- Name_YearBranch_Roll No (Eg: abc_2MEE_*)
  • Entries will be accepted via a google form only. Make sure to use your Thapar Id
  • Any kind of offensive content or vulgarity is strictly prohibited
  • Entries must be submitted before the deadline i.e. 10th December
  • Disobedience of rules will lead to disqualification
